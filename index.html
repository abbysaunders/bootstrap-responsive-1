A list of the top 4 cutest pictures of her

Her Story

Winnie was born as a feral cat on October 5th 2021 in Southampton County Virginia. My mom rescued her from the street and gave her to me as a 20th birthday gift!

Some of my favorite qualities of hers include:

  • Missing a toe on both of her front feet
  • Requires her face to be washed every night before bed
  • Favorite toy is a giant stuffed carrot that is the same size as her
  • Insists on sleeping on my chest every single night

Her Fall Bandana

The weekend of fall break, my roommate and I stopped by the Harrisonburg Farmers Market. There was the CUTEST stand called Thistle Hill and the lady hand makes pet bandanas. I ended up buying her this cute fall bandana. When I got home I took this photo and it became an absoulte FAVORITE!

Sweater Weather

When I moved into an old house downtown, one of the downsides was no central heat and AC. So when the weather started getting colder, I needed to find a way to keep Winnie warm. I ended up getting this super cute sweater from Amazon and she loves it!

Master of Disguise

One of Winnie's favorite things to do is jump out at me when I walk into a room. I had left some papers on my bed and when I walked in and saw her trying to hide I couldn't help but take a picture of her!

All tuckered out

Winnie often acts like she has the toughest life of all time. I guess getting told shes the cutest cat in the world and that shes so loved everyday gets exshusting. One day we were taking a nap together and when I moved it woke her up from what looks like the most restful sleep ever had!
